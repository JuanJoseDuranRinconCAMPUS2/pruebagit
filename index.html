The Resurge Of The Earth
Game Instructions History Credits
¡A New shoot 'em up!

In a world transformed by the human hand, in a place where greed and avarice control the world, a world where human action was gradually polluting the world in the search for black gold "Oil", humans in their quest for this precious resource ended up seeking help from dark forces, this generating a curse which made that precious resource would become monsters which would seek to pollute everything. In search of a last help to stop everything, nature invokes its protector and Goddess Physis so that with arrow and bow in hand she fights against this terrible contamination. Here you are the player, you can help Physis to stop this disaster, you can move around the stage avoiding that these oil stains hit you or damage the forest that you must protect. At your help are the elemental badges, mysterious powers which will help Physis to defeat the enemies.

Remember she needs your help!, It's in your hands to save this world!
